Gameplay Breakdown

Pick Your Hero

Each of the eight Hero's feature their own unique Super that activates when tuned to their current coloured zone.

Each Super changes the way the game is played and opens up new options for you and the team. What team combos can you discover?

Every Hero starts with ten units of space-time Power, but be careful! Exhausting your Power will mean the end of the hero and the game.

Enter The Rift and Place Your Zones

As you move your hero between Zones, you have the opportunity to combine Zone Gates, the doorways of space and time.

Depending on the created gate's formation, you may have the opportunity to pick up a valuable Reward or be forced to take a Risk.

A Typical Turn:

  1. Draw a Zone Card: At the start of your turn, draw a Zone card from the deck. This card represents a piece of the Rift, ready to be explored.
  2. Place the Zone Card: Choose a position on the board adjacent to an existing Zone. Align the Zone Gates to form connections with other Zones.
  3. Resolve the Zone: If the placed Zone card completes a Gate, resolve its effect:
    • Reward: Take a card from the reward deck and gain benefits like additional Power, Relics, or special abilities.
    • Risk: Take a card from the risk deck and encounter threats like Foes or Shocks that may weaken your Hero.
  4. Move Your Hero: Move your Hero token to an adjacent Zone. If you pass through a Gate that matches your Hero's color, activate their unique ability for an advantage.
  5. Check for Relics or Threats: If the reward card is a Relic, claim it and add its effects to your Hero's abilities. If a risk card is revealed, face its consequences.
  6. End Your Turn: Pass the turn to the next player and prepare for the Rift to evolve further!

Key Tips for Players:

  • Strategise your Zone placements to maximise Rewards and avoid Risks.
  • Use your Hero's unique abilities to support your team and navigate the Rift efficiently.
  • Balance exploring the Rift with protecting your Hero's Power to ensure survival.

Find the Rewards

The Reward deck consists of Recharge and Relic cards

Recharge cards are crucial in replenishing your Hero's power

Finding a powerful Relic will enhance your Hero's capabilities and open the way to your team's combined victory.

Avoid the Risks

The Risk deck consists of Shock and Foe cards

Drawing a Shock card will damage your Hero's power

Encountered Foe's can unpredictably affect your hero and their inventory. Beware the lurking Time Guardian, protector of the rift!

Escape the Rift

Find the Rift Gate to escape the Rift

Your Hero will take damage for each Zone in your hand that is not of their colour when exiting the Rift, try to remove them before the Exit Portal is revealed.
